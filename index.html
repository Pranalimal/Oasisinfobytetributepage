About Hruta Durgule

Hruta Durgule (born 12 September 1993) is an Indian actress who predominantly works in television and films productions mainly in Marathi language. She is the recipient of several accolades including, a Filmfare Marathi Award & has established herself as one of the most popular Marathi television actresses.
